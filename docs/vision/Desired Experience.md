# Desired Experience

## Summary
A living side project that rewards periodic curiosity, reveals new patterns after time away, and stays lightweight enough for quick one-hour bursts of tinkering.

## Desired Qualities
- Feels alive and surprising even when idle, encouraging return visits.
- Simple rules combine into evolving dynamics worth observing and sharing.
- Improvements ship in small, satisfying increments without ceremony.
- Functions as a playful playground rather than a job or content treadmill.

## Observations
- [feeling] Delight comes from witnessing change generated without intervention.
- [practice] Re-engaging after downtime should feel like reading a continuing story.
- [constraint] Heavy process or content work would drain motivation.
